## Debugging notes
- Use awk, not grep, on logs/train.log (grep was returning empty for some reason)
- Use `python3 -u` for any long-running process writing to a file — otherwise stdout buffers and progress appears frozen
- After kill, workers leak semaphores (harmless) and BrokenPipeErrors cascade (harmless)
